Dr. Carrie Dougherty, DMD
FOUNDER OF STAPLETON DENTAL

Doctor Carrie Dougherty is a different kind of dentist. Her gentle approach, excellent clinical skills and her commitment to patient education will make you and your family feel comfortable and at ease in the dental chair.

A Colorado State University graduate with a degree in biochemistry, Dr. Dougherty went on to attend Boston University School of Dental Medicine. Desiring more expertise in general dentistry, Dr. Dougherty returned to Colorado and completed a rigorous General Practice Residency at Denver Health Medical Center.

Dr. Dougherty is a Colorado native and she and her family live right here in Stapleton. Her husband, Sean, is a software engineer. They have three boys, Jack, Cael, and Liam.
