OUR TEAM
Dr Blake Weber

Having been taught to reply “orthodontist” at a very young age to the “what do you want to be when you grow up” question, Dr Blake Weber was inspired to study dentistry early on. He earned his DDS degree from the University of Tennessee. Prior to that, he received his Bachelors in Zoology from the University of Arkansas.

Dr Blake had a highly respected dental practice in Little Rock, Arkansas for over 30 years. He loves connecting and forming genuine relationships with his patients. He practices all aspects of dentistry with patients of all ages with a special interest in cosmetic and restorative dentistry.

During his time away from the office, you’ll find Dr Blake hiking, skiing, camping, or fly fishing, often with his partner Valerie. Blake is dedicated to his family and children Paul, Hunter, and Jessica.

Dr Hunter Weber

Originally from Little Rock, Arkansas, Dr Hunter Weber received his Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB) where he played collegiate soccer. Following undergrad, Dr Hunter received his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Tennessee in Memphis, Tennessee. He is thrilled to be a part of the awesome Dental Solutions of Central Park team and can’t wait to form lasting relationships with all current and future patients! Hunter loves spending his free time outdoors hiking, camping, fishing, and playing soccer with his wife Mary Catherine who is an ICU nurse at UC Health. He is also a sports fanatic. Their family is completed by their Persian cat, Snow, and their Sheepadoodle dog, Appa.
